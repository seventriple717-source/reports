Competitive Risk Assessment: AI Search Disruption
Microsoft Bing + Copilot: Despite $13B+ AI investment and ChatGPT integration, Bing remains at ~4% global market share. Google's queries hit all-time highs in Q1 2026, suggesting AI integration is enhancing rather than disrupting Search demand.
OpenAI / ChatGPT: Bing reached 1B MAU milestone, but primarily incremental to existing search, not replacing Google. Google AI Mode (launched May 2025 US, July 2025 UK) is counteracting with native AI search.
AWS vs GCP: AWS holds ~32% cloud share, GCP ~11%. However, GCP's growth rate (+63%) vastly outpaces AWS (~17%) and Azure (~30%), suggesting share gain acceleration driven by AI workloads.
Key risk: Long-term search monetization of AI-generated answers (zero-click risk for advertisers) remains the structural threat to watch. Currently, Search RPM (revenue per mille) appears stable.
